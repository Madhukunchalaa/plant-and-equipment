Material Damage

Comprehensive protection for your plant and machinery against accidental damage, fire, storm, and collision. Whether on-site or in storage, your assets are covered.

Public Liability

Essential coverage for third-party property damage or personal injury caused by your operations. We offer limits ranging from $10M to $50M to satisfy all site contract requirements.

Theft & Malicious Damage

Protect your fleet against theft and vandalism. Our policies include coverage for accessories and modifications, ensuring you aren't out of pocket if the worst happens.

Coverage for your machinery while it's being transported. Includes loading and unloading risks, carrier liability, and fully registered road risk for floating plant.

Blanket coverage for machinery you hire in. Never worry about forgetting to insure a short-term rental again. We cover you for the replacement value of hired equipment.
