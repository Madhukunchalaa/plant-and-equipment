Steps to Lodge a Claim

1

Secure the Site

Take all reasonable steps to prevent further loss or damage. Ensure the safety of all personnel.

2

Document Everything

Take photos of the damage. Obtain details of any third parties involved (names, contacts, license details). Do not admit liability.

3

Contact Us

Call us or email claims@plantcover.com.au with your policy number and incident details. We will manage the lodgement with the insurer.
