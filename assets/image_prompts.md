# Banner Image Prompts
We need **6 High-Quality Images** (Landscape orientation, 16:9 aspect ratio).
*Style*: Professional, modern, bright daylight, Australian construction context, high definition.

## 1. Home Page Hero Banner
**Prompt:**
> Cinematic wide shot of a busy Australian civil construction site at sunrise. In the foreground, a modern yellow excavator and a bulldozer are parked on polished dirt. In the background, blurred construction activity and blue sky. Professional photography, high contrast, sharp details, 8k resolution.

## 2. Earthmoving Insurance Banner
**Prompt:**
> Dynamic action shot of heavy earthmoving machinery in operation. A large grader and a scraper working on a road construction project. Dust kicking up slightly, golden hour lighting, powerful industrial vibe.

## 3. Excavator Insurance Banner
**Prompt:**
> Close-up profile shot of a modern hydraulic excavator bucket digging into soil. Focus on the power and hydraulics. The machine is yellow. Background is a blurred construction site.

## 4. Bulldozer Insurance Banner
**Prompt:**
> Front-facing low-angle shot of a massive crawler bulldozer pushing a mound of earth. Intimidating and powerful stance. The tracks are clearly visible. Bright daylight.

## 5. Skid Steer Insurance Banner
**Prompt:**
> A compact yellow skid steer loader working in a residential landscaping setting. Paving stones and green grass visible. Clean, modern equipment.

## 6. General/Claims/About Banner
**Prompt:**
> A friendly professional insurance broker involves in a discussion with a construction site manager wearing high-vis gear and a hard hat. They are standing on a site, looking at a tablet. Trustworthy, professional, warm lighting, human connection focus.
